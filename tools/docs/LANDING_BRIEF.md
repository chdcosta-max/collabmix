# Mix//Sync Landing Page V1 — Creative Brief

Status: Brief locked June 10, 2026. V1 = pre-community brochure.
V2 (live-venue homepage) defined in SOCIAL_VISION.md Phase 4.

## Job of the page
One job: make a DJ feel the magic moment and sign up. The magic
moment = two DJs, two cities, one mix.

## Audience
Primary: pro/intermediate DJs (Rekordbox/Serato users) who want to
mix with friends remotely. Secondary: aspiring DJs intimidated by
pro-tool complexity.

## Tone
The talented friend who's been DJing ten years. Confident, warm,
zero hype-speak. Quiet pro. Show, don't shout.

## Structure (single page)
1. HERO — the product mixing, not feature bullets. Two deck cards
   side by side, waveforms alive, DJ names + cities visible
   (Seattle / Denver), sync indicator between them.
   Headline locked from June 10 mock: "Two DJs. Two cities. One
   mix." Subline: "The booth is wherever you both are." One CTA:
   Get early access.
2. THE MOMENT — 2-3 sentences on what remote B2B is. No jargon.
3. HOW IT WORKS — three steps, one line each: bring your library →
   invite your partner → mix in real time. Rekordbox-companion
   positioning here ("your library, your cues, your grids —
   nothing to rebuild").
4. PROOF OF CRAFT — one beautiful waveform/deck UI shot. The
   waveform IS the credibility shot (why Slice B/C precedes
   marketing). Mock demonstrates three-tier grid hierarchy + bar
   counter.
5. CLOSING CTA — repeat the one action.

## Design system
Inherits app: pure black, white three-tier text (0.9/0.6/0.3),
deck blue #2E86DE / purple #A855F7 as only color moments, serif
reserved for wordmark, JetBrains Mono for data, sentence case,
generous negative space.

## Anti-goals
No feature grids, no pricing tables (pre-launch), no fake
testimonials, no stock DJ photos, no neon, no "live now" sections
until Phase 4.
